The Story Behind Pictorous

Hello! I’m Alishba — a dreamer with a camera and a deep love for telling stories through light and lens. What started as a hobby has grown into Pictorous, a space where moments are turned into art.

From golden hour portraits to quiet landscapes and chaotic wedding days, I find beauty in the real, the raw, and the in-between. Every click is more than a photo — it’s a memory, a mood, a message.

If you're someone who cherishes natural beauty, meaningful visuals, and aesthetic storytelling, then I’d love to frame your world with mine.

— Alishba Mir, Founder of Pictorous
